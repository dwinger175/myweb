Mafia Moniker

Mafia Moniker is a ttrpg I am running. It takes place in an alterante version of Modern Day New York. In this world , a few people are born with an epithet, which is a word that tied to their soul. These epithets give them magical powers, ranging from making pianos out of thin air to controlling gravity itself.

Player Characters

Jack Daniels

  • Epithet - Drink
  • Powers - Drinking beverages give him different powers.

Jack comes from a very religious family that follows its rules to an extreme. One of those rules is to abstaine from alcohol. When Jack found out about his epithet, he hid it to not incur the wrath of his parents. Trying out his epithet in secret, he found a love for drinks and beverages and learned how to make them. When his parents discovered his epithet and what he was doing , they kicked him to the street to not be seen as unholy. Now Jack has joined the mafia to earn enough money to open his own bar.

Dion Demask

  • Epithet - Play
  • Powers - Can create items found in a theater an do dance moves with magical affects

Dion has always had a love of the arts, and when he discovered his epithet, it only amplified his want to become a performer. Sadly, he belonged to a lower class family who could not afford tuiton to a performing arts college. To pay for it, Dion has decided to join the mafia. His parents are fine with this and fully support his decision.
